# Courtside — App Store Icon Prompts for Gemini

Use these prompts in Gemini's image generation. Each targets a different visual direction while keeping the bold, sporty basketball feel.

---

## Prompt 1 — Dynamic Basketball in Motion

> Design a bold, sporty app store icon for a basketball app called "Courtside." Show a basketball mid-bounce with motion lines and energy streaks against a dark background. The basketball should have a stylized, modern look with vibrant orange and electric highlights. Strong contrast, no text, rounded square format, clean edges suitable for iOS and Android app stores. Professional, high-quality digital illustration.

---

## Prompt 2 — Hoop & Net Close-Up

> Create an app store icon for a basketball app called "Courtside." Feature a dramatic close-up of a basketball dropping through a net, viewed from below looking up. Bold, saturated colors with a gradient background that transitions from deep navy to fiery orange. Athletic, powerful energy. No text, rounded square format, sharp detail, suitable for app store display at small sizes. Modern digital art style.

---

## Prompt 3 — Court Lines & Ball

> Design an app icon for "Courtside," a basketball app. Show a top-down view of a basketball resting on hardwood court lines — the half-court circle and key lines should frame the ball. Bold graphic style with strong geometric shapes. Rich wood tones, bright orange ball, and crisp white court markings. No text, rounded square format, clean and iconic so it reads well at small sizes. Flat design with subtle depth.

---

## Prompt 4 — Fiery Slam Dunk Silhouette

> Create an app store icon for "Courtside." Feature a silhouette of a player mid-slam-dunk against an explosive burst of color — think flames, sparks, and energy radiating outward. Dark background with vivid orange, red, and gold accents. Bold, athletic, high-energy composition. No text, rounded square format, designed to stand out on an app store page. Modern sports illustration style.

---

## Prompt 5 — Minimalist Ball with Bold Accent

> Design a clean but bold app store icon for "Courtside." Center a stylized basketball icon with exaggerated seam lines on a solid deep-colored background (dark teal, navy, or charcoal). Add one dynamic accent element — a swoosh, spark, or speed line — in a contrasting bright color to give it energy. Sporty and modern, no text, rounded square format, designed to be instantly recognizable at any size. Flat design with a premium feel.

---

## Tips for Best Results

- **Run each prompt 2-3 times** — Gemini will give different variations each run.
- **Iterate** — take the best output and refine: "Make the basketball larger," "Darken the background," "Add more contrast."
- **Final sizing** — App Store requires 1024×1024 px. Ask Gemini for a square 1:1 aspect ratio.
- **No text rule** — These prompts avoid text since small icons make text unreadable. Add your app name in App Store metadata instead.
